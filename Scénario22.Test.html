Boeuf Bourguignon

Category:Traditional

Budget:60 Eur

Cooking Time:1hours

Preparation Time:4hours

Level:easy

Number of Servings:4

Rating:4 stars

Ingredients:

  • Beef: 1.5 kilogram
  • Bacon: 200 gram
  • Butter: 60 gram
  • Onions: 10 quantity
  • Carrot: 2 quantity
  • Garlic: 2 quantity
  • Flour: 40 gram
  • Red Wine: 0.50 liter
  • Broth: 0.50 liter
  • Mushrooms: 250 gram

Steps:

  1. Cut beef into 3 to 4 cm cubes. Peel onions, carrots, and garlic.
  2. Brown onions and bacon in a large casserole dish.
  3. Add meat and carrots, then sprinkle with flour.
  4. Pour in the stock, red wine, bouquet garni, and crushed garlic cloves.
  5. Simmer gently for 3 hours.
  6. Add the sliced mushrooms and cook for a further half-hour.
  7. Serve with pasta or steamed potatoes.

Chocolate Cake

Category:Traditional

Budget:10 Eur

Cooking Time:30minutes

Preparation Time:10minutes

Level:easy

Number of Servings:4

Rating:5 stars

Ingredients:

  • Flour: 33.5 gram
  • Sugar: 66.5 gram
  • Butter: 66.5 gram
  • Chocolate: 133.5 gram
  • Eggs: 2 quantity

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 180Â°C (gas mark 6).
  2. In a saucepan, melt the chocolate and chopped butter over a very low heat.
  3. In a bowl, add the sugar, eggs, and flour. Mix well.
  4. Add the chocolate/butter mixture. Mix well.
  5. Butter and flour your cake tin, then pour in the cake batter.
  6. Bake for approximately 20 minutes.

Wok of Chinese Noodles with Vegetables

Category:World flavors

Budget:20 Eur

Cooking Time:10minutes

Preparation Time:5minutes

Level:middle

Number of Servings:3

Rating:3 stars

Ingredients:

  • Noodles: 200 gram
  • Olive oil: 0.1 liter
  • Ginger: 10 gram
  • Red pepper: 250 gram
  • Zucchini: 1 quantity
  • Shallot: 1 quantity

Steps:

  1. Heat the water for the pasta.
  2. Chop the garlic, ginger, and shallot.
  3. Cut the zucchini, bell pepper, and broccoli into small cubes.
  4. When the water boils, add the noodles and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Heat the oil in a wok.
  6. Quickly fry the ginger, then add the garlic and shallot.
  7. Add the zucchini pieces and cover for 3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  8. If the mixture tends to burn, add a little more water.
  9. Remove the noodles from the water, drain slightly, and add to the wok mixture.
  10. Add the bell pepper and broccoli pieces and 3 tablespoons of soy sauce.
  11. Cover for 3 minutes over medium heat.
  12. Add sashimi sauce or classic wok sauce and stir.
  13. Cook until water has almost evaporated.
  14. Serve hot in bowls with chopsticks.

Gaspacho

Category:World flavors

Budget:10 Eur

Cooking Time:0minutes

Preparation Time:15minutes

Level:easy

Number of Servings:4

Rating:4 stars

Ingredients:

  • Sugar: 5 gram
  • Olive oil: 0.2 liter
  • Balsamic vinegar: 0.1 liter
  • Green pepper: 500 gram
  • Tomato: 1 kilogram
  • Cucumber: 1 quantity
  • Garlic: 1 clove

Steps:

  1. Peel and chop the cucumber, green pepper, and garlic.
  2. In a blender, combine the cucumber, green pepper, tomatoes, garlic, and a little water.
  3. Add the balsamic vinegar, olive oil, and sugar.
  4. Blend until smooth.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving.
  7. Serve cold with a drizzle of olive oil and a sprinkle of herbs.

Express Tuna Brandade

Category:Express

Budget:15 Eur

Cooking Time:20minutes

Preparation Time:15minutes

Level:easy

Number of Servings:4

Rating:3 stars

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes: 0.5 kilogram
  • Tuna: 2 can
  • Garlic: 2 clove
  • Olive oil: 0.1 liter

Steps:

  1. Peel and boil the potatoes until tender.
  2. Mash the potatoes with the olive oil and crushed garlic.
  3. Drain and flake the tuna, then mix it into the potato mash.
  4. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with parsley or chives.

Quinoa Pancake

Category:Express

Budget:5 Eur

Cooking Time:10minutes

Preparation Time:5minutes

Level:easy

Number of Servings:4

Rating:4 stars

Ingredients:

  • Quinoa: 200 gram
  • Eggs: 2 quantity
  • Flour: 50 gram
  • Milk: 0.2 liter
  • Olive oil: 0.05 liter

Steps:

  1. Cook the quinoa according to package instructions and let cool.
  2. In a bowl, mix the cooked quinoa, eggs, and flour.
  3. Add the milk and mix until smooth.
  4. Heat oil in a non-stick frying pan.
  5. Pour small portions of the batter into the pan to form pancakes.
  6. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  7. Serve warm with a topping of your choice.

Raspberry Foam

Category:Light

Budget:8 Eur

Cooking Time:0minutes

Preparation Time:10minutes

Level:easy

Number of Servings:4

Rating:5 stars

Ingredients:

  • Raspberries: 250 gram
  • Sugar: 50 gram
  • Egg whites: 2 quantity

Steps:

  1. Puree the raspberries with the sugar.
  2. Whisk the egg whites until stiff peaks form.
  3. Gently fold the raspberry puree into the egg whites.
  4. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving.
  5. Serve in individual glasses, garnished with fresh raspberries or mint leaves.

Chicory and Ham

Category:Light

Budget:12 Eur

Cooking Time:20minutes

Preparation Time:10minutes

Level:easy

Number of Servings:4

Rating:4 stars

Ingredients:

  • Chicory: 4 quantity
  • Ham: 4 slice
  • Butter: 30 gram
  • Cheese: 100 gram

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 180Â°C (gas mark 6).
  2. Cook the chicory in salted water for 10 minutes, then drain.
  3. Wrap each chicory in a slice of ham and place in a baking dish.
  4. In a saucepan, melt the butter, add flour, and cook for a minute.
  5. Gradually add milk, stirring constantly until thickened.
  6. Pour the sauce over the chicory and ham, and sprinkle with grated cheese.
  7. Bake in the oven for 20 minutes until golden brown.
