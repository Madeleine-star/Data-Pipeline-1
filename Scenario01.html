Boeuf Bourguignon

Category:Traditional

Type:main course

Budget:60 Eur

Cooking Time:1hours

Preparation Time:4hours

Level:easy

Number of Servings:4

Rating:4 stars

Ingredients:

  • Beef: 1.5 kilogram
  • Bacon: 200 gram
  • Butter: 60 gram
  • Onions: 10 quantity
  • Carotte: 2 quantity
  • Garlic: 2 quantity
  • Flour: 40 gram
  • Red Wine: 0.50 liter
  • Broth: 0.50 liter
  • Mushrooms: 250 gram

Steps:

  1. Cut beef into 3 to 4 cm cubes. Peel onions, carrots, and garlic.
  2. Brown onions and bacon in a large casserole dish.
  3. Add meat and carrots, then sprinkle with flour.
  4. Pour in the stock, red wine, bouquet garni, and crushed garlic cloves.
  5. Simmer gently for 3 hours.
  6. Add the sliced mushrooms and cook for a further half-hour.
  7. Serve with pasta or steamed potatoes.

Wok of Chinese Noodles with Vegetables

Category:World flavors

Type:main course

Budget:20 Eur

Cooking Time:10minutes

Preparation Time:5minutes

Level:middle

Number of Servings:3

Rating:3 stars

Ingredients:

  • Noodles: 200 gram
  • Olive oil: 0.1 liter
  • Ginger: 10 gram
  • Red pepper: 250 gram
  • Zucchini: 1 quantity
  • Shallot: 1 quantity

Steps:

  1. Heat the water for the pasta.
  2. Chop the garlic, ginger, and shallot.
  3. Cut the zucchini, bell pepper, and broccoli into small cubes.
  4. When the water boils, add the noodles and cook for 5 minutes, or as long as you normally cook your noodles. They should not be too soft.
  5. Heat the oil in a wok.
  6. When it's very hot, quickly fry the ginger, then add the garlic and shallot.
  7. Add the zucchini pieces and cover for 3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  8. If the mixture tends to burn, add a little more water.
  9. Remove the noodles from the water, drain slightly, and add to the wok mixture.
  10. Add the bell pepper and broccoli pieces and 3 tablespoons of soy sauce.
  11. Cover for 3 minutes over medium heat.
  12. Add sashimi sauce or classic wok sauce and stir.
  13. Cook until water has almost evaporated.
  14. Serve hot in bowls with chopsticks.

Raspberry Foam

Category:Light

Type:dessert

Budget:20 Eur

Cooking Time:2minutes

Preparation Time:20minutes

Level:expert

Number of Servings:2

Rating:5 stars

Ingredients:

  • Raspberry: 86.5 gram
  • Sugar: 30 gram
  • Eggs: 1 quantity
  • Lemon: 0.1 liter

Steps:

  1. Blend the raspberries and strain to remove the seeds.
  2. Add a dash of lemon juice to soften the acidity.
  3. Add 10 g of sugar to the raspberry pulp and blend. Set aside.
  4. Start whipping the egg whites until stiff. At the same time, prepare a sugar syrup with 30 g water and 80 g sugar.
  5. As soon as the mixture begins to bubble, stop cooking.
  6. Drizzle in the sugar syrup while continuing to whisk the egg whites. The texture should be smooth and shiny.
  7. Incorporate the raspberry pulp and mix again until you obtain an airy mousse.
