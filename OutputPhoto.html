Boeuf Bourguignon

Category: Traditional
Budget: 60Eur
Cooking Time: 1hours, Preparation Time: 4hours
Level: easy
Servings: 4
Rating: 4stars

Ingredients:

  • Beef - 1.5kilogram
  • Bacon - 200gram
  • Butter - 60gram
  • Onions - 10quantity
  • Carrot - 2quantity
  • Garlic - 2quantity
  • Flour - 40gram
  • Red Wine - 0.50liter
  • Broth - 0.50liter
  • Mushrooms - 250gram

Steps:

Cut beef into 3 to 4 cm cubes. Peel onions, carrots, and garlic.

Brown onions and bacon in a large casserole dish.

Add meat and carrots, then sprinkle with flour.

Pour in the stock, red wine, bouquet garni, and crushed garlic cloves.

Simmer gently for 3 hours.

Add the sliced mushrooms and cook for a further half-hour.

Serve with pasta or steamed potatoes.

Wok of Chinese Noodles with Vegetables

Category: World flavors
Budget: 20Eur
Cooking Time: 10min, Preparation Time: 5min
Level: middle
Servings: 3
Rating: 3stars

Ingredients:

  • Noodles - 200gram
  • Olive oil - 0.1liter
  • Ginger - 10gram
  • Red pepper - 250gram
  • Zucchini - 1quantity
  • Shallot - 1quantity

Steps:

Heat the water for the pasta.

Chop the garlic, ginger, and shallot.

Cut the zucchini, bell pepper, and broccoli into small cubes.

When the water boils, add the noodles and cook for 5 minutes.

Heat the oil in a wok.

Quickly fry the ginger, then add the garlic and shallot.

Add the zucchini pieces and cover for 3 minutes, stirring occasionally.

If the mixture tends to burn, add a little more water.

Remove the noodles from the water, drain slightly, and add to the wok mixture.

Add the bell pepper and broccoli pieces and 3 tablespoons of soy sauce.

Cover for 3 minutes over medium heat.

Add sashimi sauce or classic wok sauce and stir.

Cook until water has almost evaporated.

Serve hot in bowls with chopsticks.

Gaspacho

Category: Soup
Budget: 10Eur
Cooking Time: 0min, Preparation Time: 15min
Level: easy
Servings: 4
Rating: 4stars

Ingredients:

  • Sugar - 5gram
  • Olive oil - 0.2liter
  • Balsamic vinegar - 0.1liter
  • Green pepper - 500gram
  • Tomato - 1kilogram
  • Cucumber - 1quantity
  • Garlic - 1clove

Steps:

Peel and chop the cucumber, green pepper, and garlic.

In a blender, combine the cucumber, green pepper, tomatoes, garlic, and a little water.

Add the balsamic vinegar, olive oil, and sugar.

Blend until smooth.

Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving.

Serve cold with a drizzle of olive oil and a sprinkle of herbs.

Express Tuna Brandade

Category: Traditional
Budget: 15Eur

Level: easy
Servings: 4
Rating: 3stars

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes - 0.5kilogram
  • Tuna - 2can
  • Garlic - 2clove
  • Olive oil - 0.1liter

Steps:

Peel and boil the potatoes until tender.

Mash the potatoes with the olive oil and crushed garlic.

Drain and flake the tuna, then mix it into the potato mash.

Serve hot, garnished with parsley or chives.

Quinoa Pancake

Category: Healthy
Budget: 5Eur
Cooking Time: 10min, Preparation Time: 5min
Level: easy
Servings: 4
Rating: 4stars

Ingredients:

  • Quinoa - 200gram
  • Eggs - 2quantity
  • Flour - 50gram
  • Milk - 0.2liter
  • Olive oil - 0.05liter

Steps:

Cook the quinoa according to package instructions and let cool.

In a bowl, mix the cooked quinoa, eggs, and flour.

Add the milk and mix until smooth.

Heat oil in a non-stick frying pan.

Pour small portions of the batter into the pan to form pancakes.

Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown.

Serve warm with a topping of your choice.

Raspberry Foam

Category: Dessert
Budget: 8Eur
Cooking Time: 0min, Preparation Time: 10min
Level: easy
Servings: 4
Rating: 5stars

Ingredients:

  • Raspberries - 250gram
  • Sugar - 50gram
  • Egg whites - 2quantity

Steps:

Puree the raspberries with the sugar.

Whisk the egg whites until stiff peaks form.

Gently fold the raspberry puree into the egg whites.

Chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving.

Serve in individual glasses, garnished with fresh raspberries or mint leaves.

Chicory and Ham

Category: Traditional
Budget: 12Eur
Cooking Time: 20min, Preparation Time: 10min
Level: easy
Servings: 4
Rating: 4stars

Ingredients:

  • Chicory - 4quantity
  • Ham - 4slice
  • Butter - 30gram
  • Cheese - 100gram

Steps:

Preheat your oven to 180Â°C (gas mark 6).

Cook the chicory in salted water for 10 minutes, then drain.

Wrap each chicory in a slice of ham and place in a baking dish.

In a saucepan, melt the butter, add flour, and cook for a minute.

Gradually add milk, stirring constantly until thickened.

Pour the sauce over the chicory and ham, and sprinkle with grated cheese.

Bake in the oven for 20 minutes until golden brown.
