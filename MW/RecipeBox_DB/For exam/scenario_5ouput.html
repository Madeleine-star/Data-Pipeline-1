Recipe steps:

Main course

Recipe Name: Wok of chinese noodles with vegetables

Cooking time: 10 min - Preparation time: 5 min

Ingredient:

Ingredient quantity
Noodles 200 gram
Olive oil 0.1 liter
Ginger 10 gram
Red pepper 250 gram
Zucchini 1 quantity
Shallot 1 quantity

Recipe steps:

Heat the water for the pasta. Start by preparing the ingredients: Chop the garlic, ginger and shallot. Cut the zucchini, bell pepper and broccoli into small cubes. When the water boils, add the noodles and cook for 5 minutes, or as long as you normally cook your noodles. They should not be too soft. Heat the oil in a wok. When it's very hot, quickly fry the ginger, then add the garlic and shallot. Immediately add the zucchini pieces and cover for 3 minutes, stirring occasionally. If the mixture tends to burn, add a little more water. Remove the noodles from the water, drain slightly and add to the wok mixture. Add the bell pepper and broccoli pieces and 3 tablespoons of soy sauce. Cover for 3 minutes over medium heat. Add sashimi sauce or classic wok sauce and stir. Cook until water has almost evaporated. Serve hot in bowls with chopsticks.

Dessert

Recipe Name: Raspberry foam

Cooking time: 2 min - Preparation time: 20 min

Ingredient:

Ingredient quantity
Sugar 30 gram
Eggs 1 quantity
Lemon 0.1 liter
Raspberry 86.5 gram

Recipe steps:

Blend the raspberries and strain to remove the seeds. Add a dash of lemon juice to soften the acidity. Add 10 g of sugar to the raspberry pulp and blend. Set aside. Start whipping the egg whites until stiff. At the same time, prepare a sugar syrup with 30 g water and 80 g sugar. As soon as the mixture begins to bubble, stop cooking. Drizzle in the sugar syrup while continuing to whisk the egg whites. The texture should be smooth and shiny. Incorporate the raspberry pulp and mix again until you obtain an airy mousse.
